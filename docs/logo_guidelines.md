# Repository Logo & Favicon Guidelines

This document establishes the branding guidelines, visual concepts, and spacing rules for the `boc-allocation-review-agent` project logo and favicon.

---

## 💡 Logo Concept

The logo represents the intersection of structured accounting rules and modern agentic architectures. 
- **Symbol**: An abstract double-folded ledger sheet forming a shield or a stylized letter `A` (representing "Agent" and "Audit").
- **Core Meaning**: 
  - The horizontal grid lines represent the General Ledger (GL).
  - A glowing node in the center represents the Planner/Executor loop reviewing the data.
  - The protective shield boundary represents the deterministic, secure, offline-first safety bounds.

---

## 🎨 Specifications & Colors

### Color Codes
- **Primary Color**: Deep Indigo (`#4F46E5` / HSL 243, 75%, 59%)
- **Secondary Accent**: Electric Cyan (`#06B6D4` / HSL 189, 94%, 43%)
- **Neutral Light**: Ice White (`#F8FAFC` / HSL 210, 40%, 98%)
- **Neutral Dark**: Obsidian Slate (`#0F172A` / HSL 222, 47%, 11%)

### Spacing & Grid Rules
- **Proportions**: The icon is structured on an 8px grid system.
- **Clearance Zone**: A minimum padding of `25%` of the logo's width must surround it at all times to prevent visual crowding from other graphic elements.
- **Minimum Size**: 
  - Standard SVG: scalable up to banner formats.
  - Sidebar logo: 32px height.

---

## 🌓 Alternate Versions

### Monochrome Version
For use on solid dark or light documents, backgrounds, and terminal printouts:
- **Dark Backgrounds**: Clean flat white (`#FFFFFF`) icon.
- **Light Backgrounds**: Flat Obsidian Slate (`#0F172A`) icon.

### Favicon Usage
For browser tabs, Streamlit dashboard instances, and shortcuts:
- **Dimensions**: Standard 16x16, 32x32, or 48x48 pixel ICO or transparent PNG.
- **Simplification**: The detailed grid lines are removed, leaving only the primary shield outline and the central review dot to preserve visibility at very low resolutions.
